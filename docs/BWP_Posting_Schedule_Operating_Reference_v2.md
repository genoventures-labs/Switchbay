# Bound With Purpose Posting Schedule Operating Reference

Version 2.0 — locked July 10, 2026  
Timezone: Pacific Time

## Default Daily Schedule

Default cadence: five quality posts per day.

| Slot | Time | Format | Job | Primary KPI |
|---|---:|---|---|---|
| Morning opener | 8:15 AM **or** 10:00 AM | 8:15 text / 10:00 image | Seed the day with a short principle, reflection, or clean graphic | Reach + early engagement |
| Anchor | 12:00 PM | Photo/image first | Strongest educational or authority asset | Reach + shares |
| Reach play | 2:00 PM | Photo/image | Primary shareable standard, correction, or boundary graphic | Reach + shares |
| Authority | 4:00 PM **or** 5:00 PM | 4:00 image / 5:00 text | Choose a visual share play or a cornerstone text post | Shares or reach + comments |
| Community | 7:00 PM | Text | Specific question, reflection, green flag, repair, or trust discussion | Comments + quality clicks |

Choose one morning opener on normal days. Choose either 4:00 PM image or 5:00 PM text; do not routinely force both one hour apart.

## Surge Mode

Use when an existing post or page-day is clearly outrunning the normal baseline.

Post at:

1. 8:15 AM — short text principle
2. 10:30 AM — shareable image
3. 12:30 PM — strongest educational anchor
4. 2:30 PM — second image reach play
5. 5:00 PM — cornerstone text
6. 7:00 PM — specific community question

Six posts is the normal ceiling. Return to the default rhythm when momentum cools.

## Resource Mode

Resource days use conversation first and link delivery second.

- Earlier content: naturally teach the problem the resource solves.
- 5:00 PM: community-need or context text.
- 6:30–7:00 PM: linked image containing the actual delivery CTA.
- The linked image replaces the normal 7:00 PM discussion post.
- Resume normal reach content while link clicks continue over the next 24–72 hours.

Measure **Link Clicks** for outbound conversion. Facebook **Total Clicks** includes Other Clicks, photo clicks, and other in-platform actions.

## Operating Rules

- Default to five quality posts daily.
- Six is the normal ceiling; seven or eight requires an exceptional live event or deliberate test.
- Target 1.5–2.25 hours between posts.
- Use format-specific timing: images around noon, 2 PM, and 4 PM; text around 8 AM, 5 PM, and 7 PM.
- Treat 10 AM as a stable support slot, not the premium anchor.
- Do not ban 1 PM. It is an overflow/research slot, while 2 PM remains stronger for photos.
- Day-of-week content rotation is editorial guidance, not proof that a weekday itself is magical.
- Judge posts only after at least 72 hours.
- Review the schedule every 14 days using median reach, within-day performance, comments per 1,000 reach, shares per 1,000 reach, and Link Clicks.
- Promote a test slot only after at least four comparable posts repeat the result.

## Review Standard

Do not change the locked rhythm because of one viral post or one weak post. Re-rank timing only with mature, format-matched samples. The schedule exists to compound reach, trust, conversation, and practical utility without sacrificing the Bound With Purpose standard.
